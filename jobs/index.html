VR Application Developer (Working Student / Intern)

As a VR Application Developer at Feram, you will be responsible for developing VR apps for Oculus headsets.

This includes:

  • Requirement analysis
  • Prototyping
  • Development with Unity and Godot
  • UX testing with users
  • Distribution of apps through common app stores

Work flexibly in the way that best fits your lecture schedule. During the semester up to 20 hours per week, and during the lecture-free period up to 40 hours per week.

Your Profile

  • Highly motivated with a high level of independence
  • You want to do your best to delight users, help your team, and strive for excellence
  • Student in Computer Science or a related field
  • Prior programming experience with gaming engines
  • You know your way around on *nix operating systems
  • Most importantly: You are not scared of diving headfirst into uncharted territory
